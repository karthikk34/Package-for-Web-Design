About Us

At the Paw-sitive Change, we are dedicated to finding loving homes for pets in need. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals who have been abandoned, neglected, or abused. We believe every pet deserves a second chance, and we work hard to connect animals with caring families. Join us in making a difference!

Our team of volunteers is passionate about animal welfare and is here to assist you in finding the perfect companion. We provide resources and support to help you prepare for pet ownership and ensure a successful adoption.
